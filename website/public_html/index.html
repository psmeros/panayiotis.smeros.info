Hello, my name is Panayiotis Smeros and welcome to my personal webpage.

I obtained my BSc and MSc degree from the Department of Informatics and Telecommunications of the National and Kapodistrian University of Athens where I was also a member of the Management of Data and Information Knowledge Group.

Currently, I am doing an internship in the Distributed Information Systems Laboratory of the École Polytechnique Fédérale de Lausanne.

My research interests span the overlapping areas of Semantic Web, Link Discovery/Entity Resolution, Databases as well as Distributed Systems and Artificial Intelligence.
