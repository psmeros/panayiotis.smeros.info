Welcome

Hi, my name is Panayiotis Smeros and welcome to my personal webpage.

Currently, I am an M.Sc student at the Department of Informatics & Telecommunications (DI) of the National & Kapodistrian University of Athens (NKUA) and a member of the Management of Data and Information Knowledge Group (MaDgiK).

I also work as a scientific programmer and research assistant in the context of the European FP7 project TELEIOS and I am a member of the developing team of the semantic spatiotemporal RDF store Strabon.
