Welcome

Hi, my name is Panayiotis Smeros and welcome to my personal webpage.

Currently, I am an M.Sc student at the Department of Informatics & Telecommunications of the National & Kapodistrian University of Athens and a member of the Management of Data and Information Knowledge Group (MaDgiK).

I also work as a scientific programmer and research assistant at the Department of Informatics & Telecommunications of the National & Kapodistrian University of Athens in the context of the European FP7 projects LEO and MELODIES.
