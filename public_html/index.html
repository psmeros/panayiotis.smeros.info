Welcome

Hello, my name is Panayiotis Smeros and welcome to my personal webpage.

Currently, I am a member of the Management of Data and Information Knowledge Group at the Department of Informatics and Telecommunications of the National and Kapodistrian University of Athens and I work as a research assistant in the context of the European FP7 projects LEO and MELODIES.
